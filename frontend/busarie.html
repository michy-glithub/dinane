Bursaries & Scholarships in South Africa

Discover financial opportunities to support your studies in universities and colleges for 2026.

1. National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS)

  • Provides financial aid to eligible South African students at public universities and TVET colleges.
  • Eligibility: South African citizens with household income below R350,000 per year.
  • Application: www.nsfas.org.za

2. Funza Lushaka Bursary

  • Supports students pursuing teaching qualifications in scarce skills areas.
  • Eligibility: South African citizens under 30, registered for B.Ed or PGCE program.
  • Application: www.funzalushaka.doe.gov.za

3. StudyTrust Bursaries

  • Offers bursaries in partnership with South African and international organizations.
  • Eligibility: Academically deserving students needing financial assistance.
  • Application: studytrust.org.za

4. Investec Tertiary Bursary Programme

  • Financial support for students pursuing degrees in commerce, engineering, and science.
  • Eligibility: South African citizens with strong academic records.
  • Application: investec.com

5. Sasol Bursary Programme

  • Comprehensive financial and developmental support to students in engineering and science.
  • Eligibility: South African citizens with strong academic background.
  • Application: sasolbursaries.com

6. South African Reserve Bank (SARB) External Bursary Scheme

  • Supports undergraduate students pursuing degrees related to SARB functions.
  • Eligibility: SA students under 30 with an average of 70% in Grade 12, financial need.
  • Application: resbank.co.za

7. Netcare Bursary

  • Financial assistance to students pursuing careers in healthcare (nursing, EMS).
  • Eligibility: South African citizens committed to healthcare careers.
  • Application: zabursaries.co.za

8. Mandela Rhodes Foundation Scholarship

  • Prestigious scholarship combining financial support with leadership development.
  • Eligibility: African citizens under 30 pursuing postgraduate studies in SA.
  • Application: mandelarhodes.org

9. France-South Africa Scholarship Programme

  • Opportunities to pursue Master's or Doctoral studies in France.
  • Eligibility: South African citizens with strong academic record.
  • Application: gov.za

10. ISACA South Africa Chapter Scholarship

  • Scholarships for students pursuing information systems related degrees.
  • Eligibility: Enrolled in relevant SA programs.
  • Application: isaca.org

11. Cape Tercentenary Foundation Scholarships

  • Funding for postgraduate studies in arts, culture, and environmental sciences.
  • Eligibility: South African citizens pursuing research in specified fields.
  • Application: en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Cape_Tercentenary_Foundation
